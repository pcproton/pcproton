About PCProton

PCProton was built to remove the stress and confusion from buying a custom PC. We focus on designing builds that make sense for your budget, your games, and your goals — without pushing unnecessary parts or expensive upgrades.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike big companies that mass-produce systems, every build here is personalised. You get clean cable management, compatibility checks, and honest advice. No shortcuts, no inflated prices.

Who We Build For

Whether you’re a gamer chasing higher FPS, a student who needs a reliable computer for school, or someone who simply wants a smooth everyday machine — PCProton is here to build the perfect system for you.
